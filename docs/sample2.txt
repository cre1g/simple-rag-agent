Title: Office Renovation Schedule

The marketing department’s office will be closed for renovations from March 15 to April 5, 2026. During this time, all team members are encouraged to work remotely. Temporary workstations will be available in the shared workspace on the 3rd floor. The renovation will include new ergonomic desks, improved lighting, and updated conference rooms. IT support will be on-site to assist with any equipment setup during the transition period.
