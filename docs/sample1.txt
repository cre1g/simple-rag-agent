Title: Zephyr Smartwatch Launch

Zephyr Inc. is releasing its new Zephyr Smartwatch on June 12, 2026. The watch includes a heart rate monitor, GPS tracking, sleep analytics, and customizable watch faces. Early adopters can pre-order starting May 20 through the Zephyr website. The initial batch will be limited to 5,000 units. Customers who pre-order will also get a free subscription to Zephyr Fitness+, a companion app for guided workouts and wellness tracking.
