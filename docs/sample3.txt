Title: City Art Festival 2026

The annual City Art Festival will take place from July 3–10, 2026, at the Downtown Arts District. The festival will feature over 50 artists showcasing paintings, sculptures, photography, and digital installations. Daily workshops will cover topics like watercolor techniques, 3D printing, and interactive media. Tickets can be purchased online or at the festival entrance. Volunteers are needed to help with setup, registration, and guided tours.
